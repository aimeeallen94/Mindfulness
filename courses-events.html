Soulful Sunday Retreat

Sunday

May 28th

9am to 5pm

Cliffs Retreat Cottage


A holistic one day retreat where you are guided through a special day of soothing, nurturing, healing and inspiring classes to bring you back to your true self. Rest, Recover and Reflfect

Friday to Sunday

16th to 18th June

Cliffs Retreat Cottage


Escape, switch off and unwind at our deep healing retreat. Three days where you can take sanctuary in our safe space that we have created where you can rest, reflect and reconnect with yourself. From healthy nutritious breakfasts to start the day and yoga nidra to allow you to drift off into a deep sleep to end the day we want you to fully unwind with us. You will be guided through Vinyasa yoga, breathwork sessions and meditations You will also be provided with free time to spend as you wish, exploring our serene gardens, taking in the seaviews or enjoying a walk. You will rest, reset and develop a practise that you can take home with you to form your own regular practise.

Mountain and Meditation Morning

Saturday

10th July

Cliffs Retreat Cottage


We will begin our Mountain and Meditation Morning in our cottage with a grounding meditation and some light, freshly baked refreshments before setting out to hike one of our gorgeous nearby mountains. Here you will be given time to both take in the mountain, its views and to pay attention to your body as you exercise. We will also guide you throught a walking meditation as we move. We will return to the cottage for some gentle stretching, breathwork and a final meditation before we finish the day. We hope this short retreat provides you with the space you need to check in with yourself.

Please email aimee@themindfulway.com if you would like to attend or have any questions
